Common Inquiries

Transparent answers to your most frequent questions.

How do I book a consultation?

You can book a consultation directly through our contact page or by clicking the "Book Appointment" button. We'll match you with the artist best suited for your style.

What is your hygiene protocol?

We maintain medical-grade sterilization standards. All needles are single-use, and our workspace is thoroughly sanitized between every session. Your safety is our absolute priority.

How much does a tattoo cost?

Pricing varies based on the complexity, size, and artist. During your consultation, we'll provide a transparent quote based on the estimated time required for your custom design.

Can I bring my own design?

Absolutely. Whether you have a finished design or just a rough idea, our artists will work with you to refine it and ensure it's optimized for the best possible tattoo result.
