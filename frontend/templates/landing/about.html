Our Legacy

More than just Ink

Dragon Tattoos was founded on the principle that every tattoo is a shared journey between artist and client. We don't just mark skin; we celebrate identity.

Our Philosophy

At Dragon Tattoos, we believe in the technical mastery of the craft. Every line must be precise, every shadow must be soft, and every saturation must be perfect. Our studio is built on three pillars: Artistic Excellence, Medical-Grade Safety, and Absolute Originality.

Whether you are getting your first piece or completing a bodysuit, our commitment to quality remains unwavering. We treat every project with the same level of obsession and care.

Supreme Safety

Exceeding hospital standards for sterilization. Your health is our highest priority, utilizing top-tier barrier protection and equipment.

Custom Design

We never repeat a tattoo. Every piece is custom-drawn for the individual, ensuring your art remains as unique as your story.

Award Winning

Our artists are internationally recognized and specialized in diverse styles, from hyper-realism to ancient geometric patterns.
