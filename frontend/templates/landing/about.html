About Our Studio

Dragon Tattoos is Bengaluru's premier destination for custom body art, blending artistic mastery with uncompromising safety.

Our Philosophy

We believe that a tattoo is more than just ink on skin; it's a piece of your identity, a story told through art. Our studio was founded on the principles of creativity, precision, and respect for the medium.

Every artist at Dragon Tattoos is a specialist in their craft, dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in modern tattooing while honoring the rich history of the art form.

Uncompromising Safety

Your safety is our absolute priority. We maintain hospital-grade sterilization standards, using only the highest quality inks and world-class equipment to ensure a safe and comfortable experience.
