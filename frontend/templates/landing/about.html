Our Story

Where It All Began

Dragon Tattoos Studio was founded in 2019 by a group of passionate artists who believed that tattooing is more than just ink on skin — it is a powerful form of self-expression and storytelling.

What started as a small studio with one artist has grown into a full creative space with multiple talented artists, each specialising in different styles and techniques.

Today we are proud to have served over 500 happy clients, creating thousands of unique tattoos that tell their individual stories.
