Common Queries

Service FAQ

Do you do cover-ups of old tattoos?

Yes, we specialize in breathing new life into old ink. Cover-ups require a consultation to assess the existing artwork and design a viable solution.

Is there a minimum charge?

Our studio minimum is ₹1,000. This covers materials, sterilization, and basic setup for even the smallest designs.

How should I prepare for my session?

Stay hydrated, get plenty of sleep, and eat a good meal before arriving. Avoid alcohol for 24 hours prior to your appointment.
