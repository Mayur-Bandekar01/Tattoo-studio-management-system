Medical Safety

Your health is our ultimate priority. We operate under strict clinical guidelines, exceeding industry standards to ensure a safe, sterile environment for every procedure.

Single-Use Everything

From needles to ink caps, every component that touches your skin is 100% disposable and single-use.

Advanced Sterilization

Our studio utilizes medical-grade autoclaves and ultrasonic cleaners for non-disposable technical equipment.

Pigment Integrity

We only use world-class, REACH-compliant vegan inks that are heavy-metal free and dermatologically tested.

Aftercare Commitment

Art requires care. We provide every client with a premium aftercare kit and a 24/7 support line for healing inquiries.

Cleanse

Gently wash with unscented, antimicrobial soap twice a day. Pat dry with a fresh paper towel.

Hydrate

Apply a thin layer of recommended ointment. Avoid over-moisturizing to let the skin breathe.

Protect

Avoid direct sunlight, swimming, and heavy sweating for at least 14 days post-session.

Patience

Never pick or scratch. Let the natural healing process take its course for vivid, lasting results.
