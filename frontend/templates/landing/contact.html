Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first session.

Do you accept walk-ins?

While we prefer appointments for larger custom pieces, we do accept walk-ins for smaller designs depending on artist availability. It's always best to call first!

How much will my tattoo cost?

Pricing depends on the size, complexity, and placement of the design. We have a studio minimum of ₹2,500. Large pieces are typically charged per session or hour.

How should I prepare for my session?

Make sure you're well-rested, hydrated, and have eaten a good meal beforehand. Avoid alcohol 24 hours prior. Wear comfortable clothing that provides easy access to the tattoo area.
