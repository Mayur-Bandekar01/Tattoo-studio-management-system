Common Questions

Everything you need to know about getting your first ink with us.

What should I prepare before my appointment?

Ensure you're well-rested, hydrated, and have eaten a meal. Avoid alcohol for at least 24 hours prior. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the area being tattooed.

Do you have a minimum price?

Yes, our studio minimum is ₹1,000. This covers the setup, single-use sterilized equipment, and the artist's opening time regardless of tattoo size.

Can I bring a friend?

To maintain the highest hygiene standards and give our artists enough space to work with precision, we recommend coming alone. However, one companion is allowed in the waiting area.
