Post-Session Care

Aftercare Protocol

Follow these guidelines to protect your investment & accelerate healing

Initial 24 Hours

Critical Window

1

Protect the Fresh Ink

Keep the wrapping on for 2–4 hours. It shields your open skin from environmental bacteria and contaminants.

2

Gentle First Wash

Use lukewarm water and mild, fragrance-free soap. Always clean with your hands — never a washcloth or sponge.

3

Thin Moisturizing Layer

Apply a breathable tattoo ointment in a very thin layer. Don't suffocate the skin — less is more at this stage.

Healing Phase

Days 2–14

The Yes List

  • Wash twice daily with fragrance-free soap
  • Stay well hydrated — 2+ litres a day
  • Wear 100% breathable cotton clothing

The No List

  • No scratching, rubbing or picking
  • No swimming or prolonged bath soaking
  • No direct sunlight or UV exposure

Healing Timeline

What to expect

1–3
Days

Inflammation Stage

Slight oozing, redness and swelling is completely normal. Your immune system is doing its job.

4–7
Days

Peeling Phase

Skin peels like a sunburn. Resist the urge — peeling manually damages colour depth.

8–21
Days

Surface Healing

Skin looks healed on the surface. Keep moisturising — the deeper layers are still repairing.

21+
Days

Deep Maturation

Ink settles into the deeper skin layers. Full maturation can take up to 3 months.

Forever Care

Long-term protection

SPF 50+
Always apply before sun exposure
Hydrate
Moisturise daily to prevent fading
Touch-ups
Book annually to refresh vibrancy

Security Watch

See a doctor immediately if you notice any of the following

  • Swelling or redness that worsens after Day 3
  • Fever, chills, or flu-like symptoms post-session
  • Unusual discolouration, pus, or foul-smelling discharge
Infections are rare when aftercare is followed correctly. If in any doubt, contact your artist or visit your nearest GP. Early treatment prevents permanent damage to your tattoo.
