Initial 24 Hours

1

Protect the Fresh Ink

Keep the wrapping on for 2–4 hours. It protects your open skin from environmental bacteria.

2

Gentle First Wash

Use lukewarm water and mild, fragrance-free soap. Clean with your hands, never a washcloth.

3

Thin Moisturizing

Apply a breathable tattoo ointment. Use a very thin layer; don't suffocate the skin.

Healing (2 Weeks)

The Yes List

  • Wash twice daily
  • Stay hydrated
  • Wear 100% cotton clothing

The No List

  • No scratching or picking
  • No swimming or bath soaking
  • No direct sunlight exposure

Healing Timeline
